## Description
BPD Companion is a calm, private space for navigating intense emotions, relationship triggers, and the spirals that come with borderline traits. It is built around one simple idea: before you react, pause — understand what you are feeling, then respond in a way you will be proud of tomorrow.

Whether you are in the middle of an emotional storm or rebuilding after one, BPD Companion gives you tools that meet you where you are.

WHAT IS INSIDE

• Crisis & Grounding Mode
Step-by-step grounding, paced breathing, and a safety plan you can reach in one tap when things feel like too much.

• AI Companion
A thoughtful, non-judgmental companion you can talk to anytime. It reflects your words back with care, helps you name what you feel, and suggests skills drawn from DBT-informed practice.

• Message Guard
Paste a message before you send it. Get a read on tone, impact, and a calmer rewrite so a hard conversation does not become a rupture.

• Guided Journaling
Daily check-ins, guided prompts, weekly reports, and AI-assisted reflection that turns entries into insight instead of rumination.

• Pattern & Loop Insights
See your emotional loops, triggers, and recovery patterns over time. Catch a spiral early, notice what actually helps, and build trust in yourself.

• Relationship Copilot
Map the key people in your life, track communication patterns, and get support before, during, and after difficult conversations.

• Skills & Learning Path
Bite-sized, practical lessons on emotion regulation, distress tolerance, interpersonal effectiveness, and identity — designed for real life, not a textbook.

• Medication, Movement & Rituals
Track meds, movement, and small daily rituals that keep your nervous system steady.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your entries stay yours. Everything is designed with emotional safety and privacy in mind.

IMPORTANT
BPD Companion is a self-help tool and is not a substitute for professional care, therapy, or emergency services. If you are in crisis, please call or text 988 (US) or your local emergency line.
